Form a Club

Looking to form a new club at UC? Want to explore an interest of yours with a bunch of other UC students? We have lots of resources to get you started and help you along the way!

  • Fill out the Clubs Recognition Application form.
  • Remember to create a Constitution! Central UofT administration has some good resources to help you along.
  • If you need a budget for your club, fill out the Budget Request form.
  • Submit them all to clubs@uclit.ca
