Standing Committees Boards Commissions De Facto Standing Ad Hoc

Committees which must be struck annually as defined by Article 16 of the University College Literary and Athletic Society Constitution.

Hiring Committee

  • its ratified members hire the staff positions on Council and can assist with hiring ancillary positions as necessary.

Constitutional Review Committee

  • its members vote on proposed changes to the Constitution and Council Policy, which achieve ratification from a vote at the following Annual General Meeting.

Elections Committee

  • its members institute fair and reasonable regulations for our general and special elections in accordance with our Governing Documents.

Elections Appeals Committee

  • its members rule on appeals made during general and special elections for the Society.
